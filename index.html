Winterfest

35th Annual Winterfest

Join Us!

Join the fun at the 35th annual Children's Winterfest sponsored by the Pacific Grove Adult School Co-Op Preschools! Winterfest takes place on Saturday, December 5th 2015 from 10:00AM to 3:00PM and will be held at the Sally Griffin Senior Center in Pacific Grove at 700 Jewell Avenue across from Caledonia Park.

Gifts

This wonderful event features a Children's Store where kids can shop and buy handmade gifts for family and friends. All items are priced reasonably at $3 or less. While children check off their shopping lists, adults can browse the General Store for gifts and unique handcrafted items.

Activities

Children can participate in activities like face painting, a lollipop tree, "ice fishing" and other amusing games. Come enjoy lunch or snack at the Snow Cafe, and have a sweet treat at the Bake Sale, or enter the raffle for a chance to win terrific prizes from local businesses.

Benifit

ADMISSION IS FREE! Don't miss this charming, fun, adorable event! All proceeds benefit the Pacific Grove co-op preschools.
